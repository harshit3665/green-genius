Rabi Kharif Zayad
Zayad crops, also known as "Summer Crops" or "Intermediate Crops," are crops that are grown in India during the short duration between the Rabi (winter) and Kharif (monsoon) crop seasons. Zayad crops are typically planted in late spring and harvested in the summer months. These crops help in maximizing the use of available resources and ensure continuous agricultural activity during the offseason.
